Need Access to Real-time Driving Records?

Auto Data Direct (ADD) offers instant, real-time driving records from the Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles (DHSMV) official databases to companies qualified under Federal Driver’s Privacy Protection Act (DPPA).

Sign-up and Get Started Today

With ADD’s Florida Driver License Services, you can verify an employee’s driving history before authorizing access to valuable company assets, locate any registered vehicles associated with an individual’s Florida driver license, and confirm a customer’s current address and driver license status.
